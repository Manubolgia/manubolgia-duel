## Splendor Duel **Card Colors/Features** 

## **Key:** 

- **Turn** This ability lets you take another turn 

- **Take 2[nd] Same** This ability lets you take another gem of same color if available 

- **Steal** This ability lets you take a gem from your opponent 

- **Privilege** This ability lets you take a privilege token (from your opponent if none are available 

- **1 Color** This ability allows you to place the joker on one color column
